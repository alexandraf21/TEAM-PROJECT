Brussels Sprout Salad

Shaved Brussels sprouts make an elegant, refreshing fall salad when dressed with a simple, creamy maple vinaigrette dressing and tossed with crisp apple slices.


Ingredients:

    20-25 large brussels sprouts
    1/2 cuppumpkin seeds
    1/2 cuppecan halves, toasted
    1 large apple
    1pomegranate
    maple syrup (dressing)

Step 1

Using a mandoline or a food processor, shave brussels sprouts and place them in the bowl.

Step 2

Toss the shaved sprouts with the maple dressing and fold in the pumpkin seeds, pecans, apple slices and pomegranate seeds.
