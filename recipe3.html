Baked Gnocchi with Broccoli

Cheesy, gooey, hot and bubbly—this one-pan baked gnocchi with broccoli is 100% pure certified comfort food, and it’s ready in just about 20 minutes.


Ingredients:

    1lb gnocchi
    3 cups broccoli, florets roughly chopped
    1 cup stock (vegetable or chicken)
    4 oz cream cheese
    2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
    1 tbsp red pepper flakes (optional)

Get excited, because the meltiest, carb-iest, creamiest baked pasta dish of your dreams is just 20 minutes away. Here’s how to make it:

Step 1

Preheat oven to 400° F and bring a large pot of salted water to a rapid boil. Add broccoli and cook for 2 minutes, add the gnocchi and quickly blanch for 1 minute longer. Drain water and set aside.

Step 2

In a large oven proof skillet, bring the broth to a simmer. Add cream cheese,stirring until it’s mostly combined. Add one cup of the mozzarella cheese and stir until melted.

Step 3

Stir in the broccoli and uncooked gnocchi.

Step 4

Top with mozzarella and bake on the center rack for 15 minutes, or until the top is bubbly. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes and serve immediately. Careful, it's hot!
